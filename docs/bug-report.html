BUG-001: Inconsistent Delivery Information Display on Product Cards
Medium Severity Low Priority
Bug ID BUG-001
Summary Some product cards do not display delivery information on search results page
Reported By QA Engineer
Date Reported November 30, 2025
Status Open
Severity Medium
Priority Low
Related Test Case TC-007: Product Card Displays Required Information
Environment OS: Windows 10
Browser: Chrome (Chromium)
URL: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=wireless+mouse
Date: November 30, 2025

Description

While testing product card information display (TC-007), an inconsistency was discovered where some products show delivery information directly on the search results page product cards, while others do not display this information until the user clicks "See All Buying Options".

Steps to Reproduce

  1. Navigate to https://www.amazon.com
  2. Search for "wireless mouse"
  3. Observe product cards on the search results page
  4. Compare delivery information display across multiple products
  5. Note that some products show delivery dates (e.g., "Delivery Dec 16-29") while others show "No featured offers available"
  6. Click "See All Buying Options" on products without delivery info
  7. Observe that delivery information IS available, but only shown after this additional click

Expected Result

All product cards should consistently display delivery information on the search results page to allow users to easily compare delivery times when making purchase decisions. This is a critical factor for customer decision-making and should be presented uniformly across all products.

Actual Result

Delivery information display is inconsistent:
• Some products display delivery dates directly on product cards (e.g., "Delivery Wednesday, December 10")
• Other products show "No featured offers available" without delivery information
• Delivery information for the latter category is only revealed after clicking "See All Buying Options"

Impact

  • User Impact: Users cannot easily compare delivery times across products without additional clicks. This creates friction in the shopping experience and may lead to suboptimal purchase decisions.
  • Business Impact: Potential reduction in conversion rates due to increased cognitive load and extra steps required to access critical purchase information.
  • Frequency: Occurs consistently for products with multiple sellers or marketplace items (approximately 20-30% of search results)
  • Workaround Available: Yes - users can click "See All Buying Options" to view delivery information, but this requires additional interaction

Screenshots/Evidence

Screenshot 1: Product card WITH delivery information displayed

This product card displays delivery date directly: "Delivery Dec 16 - 29"

Screenshot 2: Product card WITHOUT delivery information (requires clicking "See All Buying Options")

This product card shows "No featured offers available" and hides delivery information until user clicks "See All Buying Options"

Suggested Fix

Display estimated delivery date for the default/featured offer on all product cards in search results. For products with multiple sellers, show delivery information for the default selected seller (e.g., Amazon.com or the Buy Box winner). This would maintain consistency with products that currently show delivery information.

Additional Notes

This appears to be more prevalent with products that have "No featured offers available" or multiple third-party sellers. Products sold and shipped by Amazon typically display delivery information consistently. The inconsistency may be intentional design for marketplace items, but from a UX perspective, it creates an inconsistent experience.
